📖 User Guide & Roles

How to Use the Panel

  • Add Players: Type names into the Manual Entry box. Press Enter or click 'Add Player'.
  • Game Setup: Choose how many Killers you want and toggle extra roles (Joker/Assassin). Ensure you have enough players!
  • Start Game: Click the Start Game button. Roles are hidden to players but visible to you. The system remembers who was a Citizen last time and gives them priority for special roles next time.
  • Voting Panel: During the Day phase, select who each player is voting for using the dropdowns. The system automatically tallies votes — the person (or "Pass") with the most votes turns Green. Ties turn Gray.
  • Kill button: Use this button to eliminate a player,either because they got voted out or got killed.

Role Directory

🔪 Killer (Mafia)
Wakes up at night to eliminate one person. They win when they equal the number of innocent citizens.
🩺 Doctor
Wakes up at night and chooses one person to heal. If the Killer targets that person, they survive.
🔍 Detective
Wakes up at night and points to one person. The Host silently nods 'Yes' or 'No' to indicate if they are a Killer.
🃏 Joker
A wild card! Their only goal is to convince the town they are guilty. If the town votes them out during the Day phase, the Joker instantly wins the game.
🎯 Assassin
Plays on the side of the innocents, but is armed with a one-time use bullet. They can choose to shoot a suspected Killer at any time. If they shoot an innocent, they waste their bullet
🧑‍🌾 Citizen
Has no special night abilities. Must use deduction during the day to find the Killers.
